Hello, my name is Jasmine! I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in May 2024 from UMass Amherst. Now, I am continuing my studies here as a graduate student. My interests are cybersecurity and low-level computing, but I also have schooling in the fields of AI/ML, natural language processing, web development, and networks. I am very interested in art and very passionate about accessible graphic design.

I was part of several organizations in my undergraduate career, including: Women in Cybersecurity (WiCyS) @ UMass Amherst Student Advisor (2023-2024), UMass Cybersecurity Club Executive Board (2023-2024), HackUMass XI Co-Director (2023-2024), and HackUMass X Organizer (2022-2023).

Art and Design

I used to do a lot of traditional painting (acrylic, etc.), traditional drawing (charcoal, pen/paper, etc.), and digital drawing (using Paint Tool SAI). More recently, I did a lot of graphic design for the extracurriculars I was in. You can see a lot of the stuff here on the UMass Cybersecurity Club Instagram.
