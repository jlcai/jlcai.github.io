Creamy Garlic Shrimp ★★★★☆
Ingredients
  1. shrimp (de-veined and de-shelled would be nice)
  2. red pepper flakes
  3. onion powder
  4. paprika
  5. salt
  6. black pepper
  7. heavy cream
  8. cheese of some sort (I use the Mexican blend)
  9. parsley
  10. onion (diced)
  11. garlic
  12. lemon juice (optional)

Cooking
  1. De-shell and de-vein a lot of shrimp
  2. Stir-fry shrimp with oil in a pan, add ingredients #2-6 (seasonings)
  3. Add butter, then remove shrimp from pan to bowl
  4. Add butter, seasonings (#2-6) again, diced onion, and garlic to pan
  5. Add heavy cream to pan
  6. Add cheese, parsley, and a tiny bit of lemon juice
Stir-fried Pork Belly ★★★★★
Ingredients
  1. pork belly
  2. 1 part mirin
  3. 1 part light soy sauce
  4. 2 parts dark soy sauce
  5. 1 tbsp garlic
  6. green scallions (optional)

Cooking
  1. Cut pork belly into smaller pieces (if it comes in long strips)
  2. Put oil in pan, put pork belly in pan, let stir fry for ~3 min
  3. Add #2-5 ingredients to pot, stir fry until get dark
  4. Add green scallions
